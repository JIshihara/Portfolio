About

I am an aspiring photographer with skills in creative design and development. My talents in photography branches out into every subdivsion, from taking the photo to editing the final product. However, photography is not my only talent, I have experience and interest in videography and movie production. Movie production is a ore extensive process that peaks my interest as well and I enjoy the difficulty and challenge, making the end product all the more satisfying.

My passions stem from a desire to create, initially I believed engineering or computer science was the way to go because it included a higher pay rate, to my disappointment I didn't find any passion in compuer science and engineering was incredibly fun but not what I wanted to do. The main point is that one cannot find their passion through a desire to make money. That is a path to a career one may or may not regret.

Divine Darkness

Under the theme of conveying a message and emotion, this photo emphasizes the fact that the subject (the leaves) doesn't need to be bright to be beautiful. I think that's true in pictures and I think that's true in life. There can't always be perpetual darkness or light, there has to be a little of both so that the other can be beautiful, which is why this picture is so powerful to me. Nature keeps its balance and this pictures shows that balance and relationship between the two. The decision to keep the sky out of focus rather than the leaves was a difficult one, but ultimately my choice was to keep the leaves in focus because it better conveyed my message of darkness and light. This photo was taken around the evening time when the pink and red hues are most abundant.

  • Camera: Canon 80D
  • Lens: 24-70mm Zoom Lens
  • Date: June 3, 2018
